Ice Fishing strategy

Practical material for those who want to play in a structured manner and without chaotic bets.

Step-by-step game session plan

  1. Determine your bankroll for the session and do not go over the limit.
  2. Choose a fixed rate of 1-2% from the bank.
  3. Record the result every 50-100 spins.
  4. Take a break after strong balance fluctuations.

Newbie mistakes

The most common mistakes are catching up, sharply increasing the bet after a loss, and playing without limits. These actions impair risk control and the quality of decisions.
